About BusPass

BusPass is a revolutionary platform allowing commuters in South Africa to load bus rides onto their access cards online, eliminating the need to visit branches. With flexible options like weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly passes, and secure EFT payment methods, BusPass makes your transit experience smoother and more convenient.
